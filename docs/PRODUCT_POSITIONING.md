# DARWIN Product Positioning

This document defines the approved outward-facing wording for DARWIN in both Chinese and English.

## Core Positioning

### Chinese

DARWIN 是一个本地优先、风险优先的 AI 交易治理系统。

### English

DARWIN is a local-first, risk-first AI trading governance system.

## Short Description

### Chinese

DARWIN 把市场识别、策略治理、真实执行、风控熔断和审计报告保持在同一条决策轨道上。

### English

DARWIN keeps market interpretation, strategy selection, live execution, risk halts, and reporting on one decision rail.

## Language Rules

- Chinese surfaces should describe DARWIN as `AI 交易治理系统`.
- English surfaces should describe DARWIN as `AI trading governance system`.
- Avoid older wording such as `自进化 AI 交易策略平台`, `trading conductor`, or contest-oriented language.
- Prefer `shadow-first lifecycle governance` over `Darwinian selection` on public product surfaces.
